Who are we?

IEC (Innovation and Entrepreneurship Community) was the first entrepreneurship center in Sudan. The center was founded by a group of talented and aspiring Sudanese Entrepreneurs.

IEC was launched in 27 April 2013, and has witnessed a new birth this Feb 2015 to become Innovation and Entrepreneurship Community with the original vision and goals.

IEC Vision

We envision a world class community driven by innovation, where our role is to support the entrepreneurial activity in Sudan with all the resources that is or will be available to us, through regular researches in the field, partnering with leading global entrepreneurship communities and bringing new opportunities to the Sudanese youth.

Our Mission

IEC is committed to educate, spread and root the entrepreneurial way of thinking in the Sudanese community as widely as it could. We also aspire to be the entity that inspires and moves Sudanese entrepreneurs by offering them the needed mentor-ship, networks and hopefully opportunities of financial support.

Our Objectives

  1. Become the main reference of statistical information of the entrepreneurial activity in Sudan.
  2. Connect the different communities, organizations as well as people of interest.
  3. Support the entrepreneurial initiatives in Sudan.
  4. Contribute to the development of regulations that improve the working environment for entrepreneurs in Sudan to lead to higher success rates.
  5. Offer Mentorship and financial support for potential ideas/startups.
